Whimsical Wonder Weaver

Think of a Context-Free Grammar (CFG) as a recipe book for making sentences in computer languages. It's like having a box of puzzle pieces where each piece is a rule in our story generator. One rule might be 'start with a name', and another could say 'add a place next'. We use these rules to build fun stories, and each rule works independently, just like puzzle pieces. That's why it's 'context-free' – the rules don’t rely on what's before or after them, they just guide us to put our story together in the right order!
